Young Adult Devotional

With the Nelson's

Sunday, May 15
3 PM - 9 PM

President & Sister Nelson have pondered over what would help Young adults navigate both this important time in their life as well as the unique times in which we live. They have prepared special messages to share with the World during a Worlwide Devotional for Young Adults. We have been invited to attend this devotional in person at the Conference Center.

What is it?

It is activities and a Devotional for Young adults in our area.
There will be food and activities before the Devotional at 6.

Where is it?

The activities will be all around Temple Square and the Devotional will be at the Conference Center.

What are the activities?

There will be a variety of activities to choose from. Including:

  • The Church History Museum
  • The Family History Library
  • The Church History Library
  • The Relief Society Building
  • Special Activities at the Social Hall Avenue Meetinghouse
  • Live Music at the Tabernacle and other Venues
    • *More information will be available on Sunday May 8th.

Schedule

  • Activities: 3PM - 6PM
  • Devotional: 6PM - 7PM
  • Activities: 7PM - 9PM

*More info will be available on Sunday May 7th.

Plan

You can drive on your own but parking will be limited

We will be riding buses with the Spanish Fork Young Single Adult ward (1138 W 100 S, Spanish Fork). We will be attending a Sacrament Meeting at 12:00PM, at 1:30 we will hop on a bus and go to Temple Square.
At 7:30 we will hop back on a bus and go back to the spanish Fork YSA stake center.
